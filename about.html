About House Meowzers Rescue

House Meowzers is a nonprofit feline rescue based in Phoenix, Arizona. Our mission is to save the lives of abandoned, homeless, and at-risk cats through compassionate care, medical treatment, and adoption into loving homes.

Since our founding, we have provided shelter, vaccinations, spay/neuter services, and adoption support to countless cats. Our dedicated volunteers and foster families make it possible for us to continue this mission.

We believe every cat deserves a chance at a healthy, happy life. With your support, we can rescue more cats and connect them with their forever families.
